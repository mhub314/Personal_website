About Me

My name is Mary Hubert. I'm from Indiana. I'm 28 years old. I currently live in Los Angeles, CA, where I've resided for 6 1/2 years. I currently work for a luxury jewelry designer as a production manager under a team of gemologists. I enjoy my current job, as it interestly ties together fashion and science. However, my fiance and I are planning to move to the east coast, where I plan to completely switch my career path to software engineering. I'm also a part time student. I'm about 3/4 of the way through with a mechanical engineering degree. I don't plan to work in mechanical engineering, although learning about it has been very interesting. For fun I like to walk, take HIIT classes, knit, and play the violin. Although I live by the beach, I don't frequently go. I do love to hike, and I'm fortunate enough to live near many hiking locations. I love the mountains, and my fiance and I are getting married in the Colorado rockies next summer.
