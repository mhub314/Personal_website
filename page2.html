Los Angeles Food Recs

These are some of my all time favorite restaurants in Los Angeles...

Jon & Vinny's

Check Out the Menu
Jon & Vinny's is a busy-busy easygoing Italian-American café. The restaurant works the wood-fired grill and blazing oven, the servers are on point, and the food is as charming as ever. There are plenty of pizzas, all of them with lightly charred edges and topped with everything from smoked ham to vodka sauce. But don’t slouch on the rest of the menu, like the refreshing salads packed with veggies or the superb, house-made pastas. Spaghetti limone with breadcrumbs is a recent highlight.

Nanbankan

Check Out the Menu

This culinary gem was founded in 1978, and for over four decades, Nanbankan has been an emblem of Japanese cuisine, captivating the taste buds of locals and visitors alike. With its longstanding presence in the heart of Los Angeles, Nanbankan has become a go-to destination for discerning diners seeking an authentic Japanese dining experience. The restaurant’s success can be attributed to the dedicated team who work tirelessly to deliver exceptional service and culinary excellence.

Baekjeong

Check Out the Menu

Baekjeong, the Korean word for “butcher,” reflects a commitment to serve only the highest quality meats and offer a truly outstanding Korean cultural dining experience, showcasing the rich authentic flavors and the unique qualities of Korean barbecue. Baekjeong is widely recognized by leading food critics as the gold standard and industry leader in Korean barbecue.
